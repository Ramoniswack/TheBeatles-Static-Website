ABOUT US

The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. The Beatles were an extremely successful rock band comprised of four. After the band's humble beginnings in Liverpool, England, the group rocketed to success in the United States following an appearance on The Ed Sullivan Show.
